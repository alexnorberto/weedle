Instructions

    Objective is to find the Pokémon which is chasing the Weedle!

  • To achieve this you will make a guess, typing a Pokémon name on the form field at the bottom.
  • Every time you make a guess, that Pokémon data will be added to the table.
  • A red background means the data does not match the correct Pokémon.
  • A green background means a match!
  • A green background means the type is correct but in the wrong position.
  • You have seven tries before the Pokémon reach the Weedle and run away from you.
  • Good luck.
